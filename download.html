    About Game

    Subway is a popular and entertaining electronic game that is part of the smartphone gaming world. Developed by Kelo Corporation, it is based on a simple and addictive idea that combines skill and speed. In this game, the player finds himself in the role of a teenage character who is trying to escape from a policeman and his dog who are chasing her for spray painting the walls. The player must pass through obstacles and encounters and collect gold coins while running and jumping over trains and avoiding collisions with different carriages and obstacles.
